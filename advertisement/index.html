Digital Card 3D Poster

Project Site

Description: Retro poster created in 3D to cross-promote the 3D digital card product with an energy drink, under the "Legion" brand.

All elements of the poster are 3D assets. Each 3d character and prop was created separately and referenced into a master file. Some material properties were overridden to give variation (e.g. Kirby colors). The 3D cards, backdrop, and text were imported from Photoshop, Illustrator, and Figma. The card images are snapshots from the digital card web page or imports from Figma.

Poster Dimensions: 7K by 19K pixels.
Model Dimensions: 50 x 50 x 24 cm (excluding backing plane)
